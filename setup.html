Morning checklist

Connecting to the Session

Open the Vyzn app 

Double tap "Start Session" on the Surface Pad and go to the session tab in Microsoft Edge

Open the Vyzn app 

Open the Vyzn app from the Main menu on each HoloLens device by doing the bloom gesture and air tapping on the Vyzn icon. After the App window appears, air tap again to place and start the app.

Look at the QR code

(Optional, may be skipped) Look at the QR code with QR scanner (it should turn green, then go away) on all devices of the group. Wait until all the messages disappear and experience appears.

Check the Location of Model

Wait for experience to appear at the saved location.
If the model is not on its place, see “Positioning the Model”.

Switch to "Welcome"

Switch the view to “Welcome” by tapping its name on Surface Pad. You may now use this group of the devices to run the experience.

Ready to go

Device is ready to use. Connect other devices by repeating the steps.

Positionning the Model

Switch the view to "Positioning"

Make sure that "Voice & Getures" are turned on for the device that you wearing

"Move Model"

Say "Move Model" if only the model is notably shifted.
Air tap when model is close to intended position.

Do the Precise Positioning by voice

Use the voice commands to move the model:
– "One/Two Inch/Feet/Centimeter/Meter Closer/Farther"
– "One/Two Inch/Feet/Centimeter/Meter Left/Right"
– "Rotate One/Two Degree"
– "Rotate One/ Two Degree Counterclockwise"
– You can also rotate by tap and slide to the side

Switch View to "Welcome"

Don't forget to turn off "Voice & Gestures". Repeat on all the devices that need adjustment.
