Morning checklist

Connecting to the Session

Open the Session page 

Double tap "Start Session" on the Surface Pad and go to the session tab in Microsoft Edge

Open the Vyzn app 

Open the Vyzn app from the Main menu on each HoloLens device by doing the bloom gesture and air tapping on the Vyzn icon. After the App window appears, air tap again to place and start the app.

Look at the QR code

Look at the QR code with QR scanner (it should turn green, then go away) on all devices of the group. Wait until all the messages disappear and experience appears.

Check the Location of Model

Wait for experience to appear at the saved location.
If the model is not on its place, see “Positioning the Model”.

Switch to "Welcome"

Switch the view to “Welcome” by tapping its name on Surface Pad. You may now use this device to run experience.

Ready to go

Connect other devices by repeating the steps.

Positionning the Model

Switch the view to "Positioning"

Make sure that "Voice & Getures" are turned on for the device that you wearing

"Move Model"

Say "Move Model" if only the model is notably shifted.
Air tap when model is close to intended position.

Do the Precise Positioning by voice

Use the voice commands to move the model:
– "One/Two Inch/Feet/Centimeter/Meter Closer/Farther"
– "One/Two Inch/Feet/Centimeter/Meter Left/Right"
– "Rotate One/Two Degree"
– "Rotate One/ Two Degree Counterclockwise"
– You can also rotate by tap and slide to the side

Switch View to "Welcome"

Don't forget to turn off "Voice & Gestures".
Repeat on all the devices that need adjustment.

Main Experience Loop

Make sure it's ready

– All the devices shall appear on the session page
– Kiosk mode is on, Voice & Gestures is off
– View is set to Welcome

Be Careful!

– Tell people not to push the band inside and use ONLY THE WHEEL to adjust its size. Otherwise the band will be broken!

Help to put HoloLens on

Ask the spectator to sit on the chair and help adjusting the device

Make sure people see all 4 corners

Ask each visitor if they see all 4 corners of “Adjust your device screen” frame and the experience title on the wall. Help them to adjust the device on their head, if they are missing some of the corners.
Explain people that they can walk and look around during the experience. Tell them that the starting part of the experience will be on the wall, then it will move to the space around the cars.

Start Experience

Press “Start Experience” button on the session page and the experience will begin.
Wait until it is over, the view will switch back to “Welcome”

Tell to carefully take off HoloLens

Tell people to take off the devices carefully by unscrewing the wheel on the back of their head.

Clean devices with the wipe

Check the charge

Check the battery level on the devices:
– If there are 1 or 2 lights, the device needs to be charged. See “Charging the devices”. Put the devices on charge and take another group of devices. See “Connecting a Device to the Session”.
– If there are 3 to 5 lights on, you can do the experience again. Repeat from begining.
– If there is nobody in line, leave the devices charging on table by connecting the Micro USB cable.

Introducing People to HoloLens


  • Full instructions on how to put on and take off the device are available at Microsoft site.
  • Don't forget to tell people not to push and not to pull the band, it will break the band!
  • Explain how to put on the device and take it off, how the band wheel works.
  • Explain the buttons and ask not to touch them.
  • Stress that the whole weight of the device should rest on the band, the device should not press on the ears or the nose.
  • Ensure that the device doesn’t press on the ears and on nose
  • Propose to move the visor closer to the person’s eyes, the experience is better when the visor is closer to the eyes.

Chraging the Devices During the Day

Connect Cable

Connect the power supply to the charging port by Micro USB cable.

Press the Power Button

Press the power button once to get the device into standby mode.
If a device is showing on the session page, it is not in standby mode.

Reconnect to the Session

After the device is charged, disconnect the cable. Find the Vyzn window in the space around you and Air Tap on it. The device will connect back to the session.

Spaces Cleaning

Space cleaning procedure may be needed when the devices tend to misplace the model inside the space. It is recommended to do in once per week.

Settings

Launch the Settings app from the main menu by air tapping on it.

Spaces

Go to System – Spaces

Remove

Press on active space and remove it

Wait

Wait until surroundings are mapped

Walk around

Walk around the space without any app launched, walk around the back wheel of yellow car a bit more.

Mobile Friendly

After start of Vyzn app you may be asked to walk around more.
